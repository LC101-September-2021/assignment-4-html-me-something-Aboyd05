My Favorite Vacation Destinations

Rome

My first time to Europe I visted Rome. I enjoyed the food and learned about the Roman empire. The coloseum was a site to see and I spent the whole day taking pictures. The first ever shopping mall was built in Rome between 107 and 110 AD by Emperor Trajan. It sold a wide variety of goods and grocery items.

Portland, Oregon

Portland was a surprise to me, I was expecting lots of rain and clouds but the weather was quite nice when I visted. Portland is a craft beer mecca and I spent most of my time visting local breweries and took a tour of a hop farm. The people were friendly and the seafood was amazing.

Phoenix, Arizona

I could see myself moving to Phoenix later in life. I prefer the warmer climate and most days it is sunny. The Phoenix botanical garden is a must do while visting. The Garden presents Spring and Fall open-air acoustic concert series, art exhibitions, and Las Noches de las Luminarias since 1978. The Luminarias Festival became a Southwestern Holiday tradition featuring live music by the flickering lights of 8000 hand-lit lamps.
